Here's the idea:

Bots are smart and very fast, but they aren't quirky.

{{demo.header}}

Who thought to take that picture? A human being!

Much like the fathers of the internet could not predict the popularity of cat videos, computers can't predict the strangess that is human behaviour.

So why can't our captcha just be a very specific question about this unexpected image? Something strange - maybe a little funny - that a bot would have a hell of a time guessing.

How about we all contribute these quirky Q/A pairs to a publicly accessible service? Sure, hackers can figure these out - they're smart people - but if we get enough continuous submissions, it'll be Sisyphean excercise!
